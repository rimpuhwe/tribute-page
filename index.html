Welcome to Rwanda: The Land of a Thousand Hills

About Rwanda

Rwanda, known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills," is a stunningly beautiful country located in East Africa. It is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and incredible biodiversity. The capital city, Kigali, is a hub of modernity and a testament to the country's remarkable progress.

TEMBERA U RWANDA Campaign

The "TEMBERA U RWANDA" campaign invites you to explore Rwanda's natural wonders and rich heritage. From the majestic Volcanoes National Park, home to endangered mountain gorillas, to the serene shores of Lake Kivu, Rwanda offers an unforgettable adventure. Discover the warmth and hospitality of the Rwandan people, and immerse yourself in the traditions and stories that make this country unique.

The "Tembera U Rwanda" campaign, launched by the Rwanda Development Board (RDB) in 2016, aims to promote domestic tourism and encourage Rwandans to explore their own country's rich cultural and natural heritage. The campaign's theme, "Tourism for All," emphasizes that tourism is not just for international visitors but for everyone, including locals.

The campaign has been successful in increasing domestic tourism and fostering a sense of pride and appreciation for Rwanda's natural and cultural treasures. It continues to inspire Rwandans to become tourists in their own country, discovering the beauty and heritage that Rwanda has to offer
